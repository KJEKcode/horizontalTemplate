My Education

After high school I attended ITT Technical Institute as a graphic design major where I met a professor by the name of Michael Grant. He taught me one of the most important lessons I would ever learn, how to be resourceful and self-educated. The next several years of my life were filled with online tutorials and documentation that sharpened my development skills.

After graduation I worked as a freelance web designer and developer which eventually lead to my first full time position in my field. With the help of my boss Bill Plampin and more online tutorial I became their Photoshop expert and while building websites for their clients.
